[  ] Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA).
[  ] Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or official websites.
[  ] Log out from shared or public devices and avoid saving passwords on them.
[  ] Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
[  ] Enable transaction alerts and regularly monitor your bank statements.
[  ] Immediately report fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
[  ] Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for urgent financial fraud reporting)
[  ] Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks (banking, logins); use mobile data or a VPN instead.
